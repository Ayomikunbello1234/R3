OUR MISSION

At R3, our mission is to make recycling simple, rewarding, and accessible for everyone.We use technology to connect individuals, communities, and brands to responsible recycling solutions—turning everyday waste into measurable environmental and social impact.

FAQS

What materials can I recycle with R3?

We accept plastics, sachets, metals, glass (in select areas), and e-waste. Accepted materials may vary by location.

How does waste pickup work?

Schedule a pickup through our platform, sort your recyclables, and we’ll handle the rest.

Do I earn rewards for recycling?

Yes. Users earn points for every verified pickup, which can be redeemed through our rewards system.

Is R3 available in my area?

We’re expanding gradually. Availability depends on your location and pickup partners.

How can brands or organizations partner with R3?

Brands can collaborate with us through CSR initiatives, community recycling programs, and impact tracking.
